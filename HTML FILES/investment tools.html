Emergency Fund Information

An emergency fund is a financial safety net for unexpected expenses or financial emergencies. It is highly recommended to have a fund equivalent to 3-6 months of your living expenses.

  • Helps cover sudden medical expenses.
  • Provides a buffer during job loss.
  • Ensures financial security in unforeseen situations.

Start saving today to build your emergency fund and ensure you're prepared for the unexpected!
